Privacy boundary

What Leafstep Pro intentionally avoids

Leafstep Pro only publishes meal guidance and pairing access. It does not become an EHR, food database, messaging system, or remote progress dashboard.

  • No patient email, full name, diagnosis, or weight history
  • No check-ins, goals, measurements, achievements, or app history uploaded
  • No calories, macros, barcode scanning, meal logging, or food database
  • Patient can disconnect and delete the local cached meal plan
