Leafstep Pro

A private meal plan workspace for nutritionists.

Leafstep Pro is a supervised-care workspace for reusable meal plans, pseudonymous patient slots, and short-lived QR pairing. The app stays private by default.

What Leafstep Pro intentionally avoids

Leafstep Pro publishes nutritionist-written meal guidance and pairing access. It stays out of check-ins, weight history, measurements, diagnoses, and messaging.

  • Use patient labels like Patient 014 instead of names or emails
  • Meal plans are guidance-only and read-only in the app
  • Dishes do not include calories, macros, barcode scanning, or nutrition database data
  • Pairing links can be revoked and recreated from the patient workspace
